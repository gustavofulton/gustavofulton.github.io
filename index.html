DriveThru Plus

Winner Grand Prize Spartahack 2015 and invitation to Global Hackathon in South Korea (which unfortunately got cancelled).

The app is made for Apple Car Play and Android Auto. How it works: Customer enters retaurant parking lot and car navigation system prompts for application to launch. After application launch, customers place order through app. Passengers in car can connect their phone to the session and they can see each others orders, but with no access to change them. If help is needed, customers can connect to a representative which will talk to them over Bluetooth. Customers pay separately and receive their orders in separate baggage. Customers receive order and enjoy!

BassLoop

Live Music Production App - Live and Working

BassLoop is a web-based music production software, where everyone in the entire world can make music at the same time, simultaneously. BassLoop uses a real-time database to manage the sounds played and sync between multiple music producers in the same lobby. Because there's no need for authentication, many different lobbies can be created and multiple people (1k+) can produce at the same time.

Runedemic

Multiplayer puzzle game

Runedemic is a multiplayer puzzle/action game where you and your opponent race against each other to try and reach the runestone in the middle of the board to power up and gain the ability to destroy each other. The power up lasts for 15 seconds and then will respawn, giving both players the chance to race for it again.
